1. Consult: Cover letter

Respected Sir/Ma'am,

My name is Devansh Jain, a third-year undergraduate student pursuing a Bachelor of Sciences in Mathematics and Computing at IIT Kharagpur. My interests lie in social impact consulting, strategy, and venture capital. I am writing to express my strong interest in the XYZ’s Internship Program and to convey my enthusiasm for contributing to your esteemed organization's mission of driving inclusive growth through strategic advisory services.

My academic journey in Mathematics and Entrepreneurship has equipped me with a robust foundation in strategic thinking, analytical capabilities, and a keen understanding of private sector dynamics.

I have actively sought opportunities to apply these skills in real-world settings, most notably during my internship as a Strategy Intern at Feeding India by Zomato, where I primarily worked on the food recovery project of the Global FoodBanking Network impacting millions of farmers to reduce their farm loss by 10-15% and increasing their incomes substantially. I have also led public-private partnerships addressing malnutrition and had the opportunity to work with the Offices of Hon'ble Governors and Hon'ble Chief Ministers, alongside multiple in-house innovation initiatives to revamp the food ecosystem in the nation further honing my strategic skills.

In addition, I have had the privilege of serving as the Asia head of the Junior IB, a student-led financial organization created with the motive to disrupt financial education and research. This role allowed me to deep dive into M&A deals, contributing significantly to my understanding of the financial landscape and enhancing my leadership and strategic skills.Furthermore, my role as the chief of staff at Getactvy, a fitness & healthtech startup featured in Forbes 100 to Watch, has been instrumental in shaping my vision for making a social impact.

In the past, I have worked as a Research Associate at Cambridge Judge Business School, focusing on strengthening Micro, Small, and medium Enterprises, and as a Strategy and Marketing Intern at Blu Ocean Private Limited, managing their celebrity collaborations including Robert Downey Jr. I have also worked at the prestigious Scheller College of Business, Georgia Tech in the finance domain.

Furthermore, my ability to collaborate across diverse sectors has been demonstrated through my involvement in the Entrepreneurship Cell at IIT Kharagpur. I facilitated the highest-ever funding(1.2 Cr+ INR) to startups and organized India’s largest Business Model competition, Empresario. Besides that, I have also excelled in numerous case study competitions such as the prestigious Tata Steel TomorrowLAB Innovation Challenge 3rd edition, Indian Case Challenge, EY Cafta Case Championship, HSBC Case Competition, and HackOn with Amazon 3.0 where I worked seamlessly with stakeholders from public, private, and philanthropic sectors to achieve common goals.

Thank you for considering my application, and I look forward to the possibility of contributing to the impactful work.

Yours sincerely,
Devansh Jain

2. Finance: Cover letter

My name is Devansh Jain, a third-year undergraduate student pursuing a Bachelor of Sciences in Mathematics and Computing at IIT Kharagpur. My interests lie in the domain of Finance and Data Analytics. I hereby write this letter to apply for an opportunity to work on a project under your company’s esteemed guidance.

I have a keen interest in Financial Markets, Commodities, and Foreign Exchange and its applications. To build my knowledge in Finance, I studied the course Probability and Statistics in my coursework. To expand my knowledge, I completed Bloomberg Market Concepts, a certification covering the basics of Economic Indicators, Currencies, Equity, and Fixed Income. I am skilled in C++, Python, and MySQL and have a lot of experience in using Microsoft Excel and Microsoft Powerpoint. I spent my time completing MOOCs in Finance like Time Value of Money by Gautam Kaul and Fundamentals of Financial and Management Accounting by Politecnico Milano.

Recently, I have worked as a Strategy Intern at Feeding India by Zomato, where I primarily worked on the food recovery project of the Global FoodBanking Network impacting millions of farmers to reduce their farm loss by 10-15% and increasing their incomes substantially. I have also led public-private partnerships addressing malnutrition and had the opportunity to work with the Offices of Hon'ble Governors and Hon'ble Chief Ministers, alongside multiple in-house innovation initiatives to revamp the food ecosystem in the nation.

In the past, I have worked as a Research Associate at Cambridge Judge Business School, focusing on strengthening Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises, and as a Strategy and Marketing Intern at Blu Ocean Private Limited, managing their celebrity collaborations including Robert Downey Jr. I have also worked at the prestigious Scheller College of Business, Georgia Tech in the finance domain.

At IIT Kharagpur, I have led the Entrepreneurship Cell, facilitating the highest-ever funding(1.2 Cr+ INR) to startups and organizing India’s largest Business Model competition, Empresario. Besides that, I have also excelled in numerous case study competitions such as the prestigious Indian Case Challenge and EY Cafta Case Championship.

I always look forward to challenging problem statements, irrespective of the field, and give my 100% to attain the outcomes and key performance indicators desired. Based on my college experience and skills, I believe I am well-suited to work under your company’s supervision due to my passion for finance, analytical abilities, writing skills, and strong interpersonal skills.

Thank you for your time and consideration.

Yours sincerely,
Devansh Jain
Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur

3. Research Internship: Cambridge Judge Business School

Dear Professor,

My name is Devansh Jain, a third-year student at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Kharagpur, pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Mathematics and Computing. I have a keen interest in Finance, Venture Capital, and Mathematics. I have taken several online courses, which have helped me develop a basic understanding of strategy and finance. I am writing to you to work under you on a project at the University of Cambridge Judge Business School. My research interests align with yours, as I am highly interested in working on a project in the empirical analysis of marketing problems at the intersection of business innovation, social impact, and marketing strategy.

Business news and how new businesses grow in a competitive environment always intrigue me. I have completed courses like Probability and Stochastic Processes, which helped me to make statistical decisions in financial planning and budgeting.

I have knowledge in the financial realm after completing the Fundamental of Financial and Management Accounting offered by Politecnico Milano at Coursera. As an Associate Manager, I also work in the Entrepreneurship Cell, at IIT Kharagpur, which is one of the largest student-run non-profit organizations in India. I have experience handling corporate relations and talking to executives of influential MNCs and domestic banks for associations. I have raised sponsorships worth x,yz,000 INR and gathered speakers and outreach partners for the events of the cell. I also handled the logistics and the execution of “Empresario” - India's largest Business Model competition with prizes worth Rs. 85 lakhs.

I love case studies; my team and I were among the Top teams of the Indian Case Challenge 2023, organized by the Business Club at IIT Kharagpur. We prepared an executive summary and investment analysis for TATA’s fab and recommended a $58.46 billion investment requirement for entry into the semiconductor industry. I want to pursue a project under your valuable guidance and learn from your esteemed leadership.

I am a focused individual and will work very hard. I shall live up to your expectations and give my best if allowed to work. I am ready to learn any prerequisites if required.

I would be deeply indebted if I received your guidance and had an opportunity to learn and apply the knowledge in the project under your direction. I have attached my Curriculum Vitae for your reference. I look forward to hearing from you.

Best Regards,
Devansh Jain
